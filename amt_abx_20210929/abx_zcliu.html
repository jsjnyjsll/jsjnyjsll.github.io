Evaluation of Synthetic Speech (Naturalness)

   The purpose of this study is to learn how to improve synthesised speech. In order to do this we are gathering people's opinions on the output of several different speech synthesis systems. You will be performing a series of evaluations of the speech produced by these systems. Please listen to the audio files below by clicking on the icons. Please first listen to the reference audio, and then listen to the evaluated audios. After that, you will choose the better audio file to be evaluated you've just heard considering the naturalness. The better audio should be more natural. You should not judge the grammar or content of the sentence, just how it sounds.Please listen with headphones..

    In order to complete the task, you had better use IE9, IE10 or IE11 browser. Please allow for showing all contents.
